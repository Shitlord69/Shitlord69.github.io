Frequently Asked Questions



How do I search for cars on your website?

By looking for the 'Buy' section, you can browse our top selling cars.

How do I schedule a test drive?

By applying an enquiry about the vehicle you are interested in, you will be directed to an agent which would assist in managing the test drive location and time.

What should I bring with me for a test drive?

Your identity card and test-drive booking invoice should be present before a test-drive.

Can I apply loan instead of directly buying it?

Yes! We will also appoint an financial consultant at a small price so that we can provide an accurate mortgage calculation.

Does Drivit accept trade-ins?

Yes! We accept trade-ins from most cars. Our consultants will guide you comprehensively throughout the whole trade-in process.

How am I sure if my car is eligible to sell or trade-in?

We will perform an inspection on your car after you appointed an inspection date and location.

How much is the booking fee?

1% of the purchase price of the car with no hidden fees.

If I change my mind, can I cancel my booking and will the fee be refunded?

Yes! We will fully refund the booking fee within three business days.

How can I access the vehicle history report?

An agent will provide you when you visit the store.

Can I negotiate the price of a car listed on your website?

No, all prices listed are fixed, except for certain sales events where prices might reduce on certain cars.

What forms of payment do you accept?

Credit card and fpx online banking only. We do not accept physical cash.

Can I purchase a car from a different location and have it shipped to me?

Yes, but a shipping fee will be charged depending on the distance of shipment.

How long is the warranty coverage?

5 years for Drivit service warranty. Another additional 2 years for premium package.

How am I sure if my new car is in good condition?

We will always perform a 175-point inspection for every single vehicle, and we will provide an inspection certificate.

How do I contact customer support if I have further questions or concerns?

By visiting the Contact Us page.
